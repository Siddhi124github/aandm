Our Expertise

Surveying the Project

  • Employ cutting-edge technology such as LiDAR and 3D mapping for comprehensive area analysis.
  • Assess and identify potential challenges or areas requiring special attention.
  • Develop and utilize in-house software for primary demographic assessment.
  • Conduct environmental impact assessments to ensure sustainability and regulatory compliance.
  • Prepare detailed reports outlining survey findings with data analytics.

Initial Engagement with Society

  • Organize introductory meetings with society members to present the redevelopment proposal overview.
  • Deliver AV presentations using survey data to enhance understanding of existing conditions.
  • Highlight social and economic benefits like improved amenities, increased property values, and enhanced community living.
  • Distribute informational brochures summarizing project impacts and benefits.

Convincing Society for Redevelopment

  • Tailor presentations addressing specific concerns from initial engagements.
  • Conduct workshops and town hall sessions educating society members on redevelopment intricacies.
  • Share success stories of similar projects to instill confidence.
  • Provide clear timelines illustrating project milestones and anticipated completion dates.
  • Establish feedback mechanisms to continuously address concerns.
  • Maintain dedicated communication channels for prompt query resolution.

Management Meeting Arrangements

  • Facilitate pre-meeting briefing sessions ensuring representatives are well-prepared.
  • Develop comprehensive agendas covering design proposals, financial considerations, and timelines.
  • Use visual aids, including 3D models and renderings, to convey redevelopment vision.
  • Encourage open dialogue fostering collaborative decision-making.

Documentation

  • Clearly outline document purposes and implications to address concerns proactively.
  • Engage legal experts assisting society members with contractual obligations.
  • Establish secure online portals for confidential document submission.
  • Conduct information sessions guiding members through signing processes with in-person and virtual options.

Communication

  • Streamline documentation via digital platforms for efficiency and accuracy.
  • Implement real-time online data storage accessible to Navbharat Mega Developers Pvt Ltd.
  • Identify key decision-makers and assign points of contact.
  • Communicate regularly with society members, keeping them informed and addressing concerns promptly.

Society Member Relocation

  • Collaborate with Navbharat Mega Developers Pvt Ltd to secure alternative housing, negotiating favorable terms.
  • Provide dedicated helpline and relocation support teams to address transition challenges.
  • Implement phased relocation strategies to minimize disruption and maintain community spirit.

Demolition and Site Clearance

  • Engage licensed demolition experts for safe and efficient processes.
  • Implement strict safety protocols and adhere to regulations.
  • Coordinate responsible waste disposal promoting environmental sustainability.
  • Secure site perimeter with 24/7 security and surveillance.
  • Maintain transparent communication with local communities regarding noise, dust, and safety.

Site Handover

  • Final handover of cleared site with comprehensive documentation including survey reports, meeting minutes, and agreements.
  • Provide a designated post-handover period to address queries or issues.
  • A&M Advisory to manage this as a turnkey project, delivering fully cleared land for redevelopment to Navbharat Mega Developers Pvt Ltd.
